Our Story

A Heritage of Flavor

Founded in 1995, Spices Garden began as a humble family kitchen in Bangalore, where our recipes were the heart of every meal.
what stared as a small eatery has grown into a beloved restaurant that serves authentic Indian cuisine to thousands of food lovers.


Our chefs are passionate about bringing the rich flavors of India to your table. We use only the finest ingredients, sourced directly from local farmers and trusted suppliers.
Each dish is crafted with care, ensuring that every bite is a journey through India's culinary heritage.


Our journey spans three generations of culinary expertise, with each dish telling a story of tradition, passion, and authentic flavors.
We source our spices directly from Indian farms and grind them fresh daily to ensure the most authentic taste experience.
